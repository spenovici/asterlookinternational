ABOUT US

WHAT IS ASTERLOOK INTERNATIONAL

Asterlook International was conceptualized on 3rd April 2013 by Anthony Basooma. The idea emerged from the high levels of poor grades and illiteracy among children from disadvantaged families due to poor education support. Asterlook International also addresses the deteriorating environment through sustainable utilization ,protection and engaging in activities that maintain and improve ecological stability hence the slogan "For Environment We Go Green And Keep It"

WHAT WE DO

Asterlook International supports children from disadvantaged background families to improve education outcomes and literacy while engaging in activities that maintain and enhance environmental sustainability, reflecting our commitment to both education and the nature.

HOW WE DO IT

Asterlook International offers fully funded scholarships to children from disadvantaged families to suppoort their education. At the same time, together with students , partners and volunteers , we engage in activities that maintain and improve ecological like making sure our students plant a tree every year.

OUR VISION & MISSION

Our vision is to empower disadvantaged children through education, while our mission focuses on creating sustainable solutions for a green environment.
