ASTERLOOK FOUNDATION

Asterlook Foundation is one of the core directorates of Asterlook International that was established to address the high levels of poor academic performance and illiteracy among children from disadvantaged families who often lack adequate educational support but have the ability to excel, protect and preserve nature.
